About Us

  • Stock Data Analysis is a student-run project that aims to provide reliable stock data and analysis to users.
  • Our platform focuses on the closing price versus date, making it easy for users to explore and analyze stock data.
  • We have used the Yahoo Finance API to fetch stock data and create graphs that visualize the trends and patterns of stock prices over time.

As a student project, we understand that our resources are limited and our dataset is restricted to the stocks available on Yahoo Finance. Despite this, we are committed to delivering a high-quality service and enhancing our platform to meet the changing needs of our users.
